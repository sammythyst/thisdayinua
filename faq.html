FAQ

What is this for?

Unus Annus may be gone but their message will always live on, and this site can help with that. "This Day In Unus Annus" is a memorial site for fans to remember all the good times. The homepage displays the video that was originally uploaded "on this day" along with The Clock counting down to the anniversary of Unus Annus ending.


Videos?

This is NOT a reupload site so there are no videos. Any reference to "video" or "episode" made on this site refers only to the components making up the original uploads including but not limited to title, description, and thumbnail. All of which are originally owned by Mark and Ethan.


The Clock?

The Clock is set to end on the anniversary of Unus Annus ending, at midnight on November 14th. During the countdown, there will be a time The Clock appears to be an hour slow. This is caused by the accommodation for Daylight Savings, where we lose an hour in the spring and gain it back in the fall. Daylight Savings 2024 will take place from March 10 to November 3.
Additionally, it's worth noting that The Clock is set to your local timezone in order to get the true "on this day" experience.


What about leap year?

Since the original span of Unus Annus took place during a leap year, there is a leap day video ("Top 10 Worst Things Your Friend Could Possibly Spend Their Money On"). However, like the other videos, it will not appear until the anniversary of its release, February 29th. The next leap year will be 2028.
