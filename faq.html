FAQ

What is this for?

Unus Annus may be gone but their message will live on, and this site can help with that. "This Day In Unus Annus" is a memorial site for fans to remember the good times. The homepage displays the video that was originally uploaded "on this day" along with The Clock counting down to the anniversary of Unus Annus ending.


Videos?

This is NOT a reupload site so there are no videos. Any reference to "video" or "episode" made on this site refers to the components making up the original uploads including but not limited to title, description, and thumbnail. All of which are originally owned by Mark and Ethan.


Why is the clock an hour off?

The Clock is set to end on the anniversary of Unus Annus ending, at midnight on November 14th. During the countdown, the Clock may appear to be an hour slow. This is caused by the accommodation for Daylight Savings, where we gain one hour in the fall.


What about leap year?

Since the original span of Unus Annus took place during a leap year, there is a leap day video ("Top 10 Worst Things Your Friend Could Possibly Spend Their Money On"). However, like the other videos, it will not appear until the anniversary of its release, February 29th. The next leap year will be 2024.
